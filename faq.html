Property Management Questions

What types of properties do you manage? +

We manage a variety of residential and commercial properties including:

  • Residential: Bedsitters, 1-3+ bedroom apartments, villas, townhouses
  • Commercial: Office spaces, retail shops, storage units
  • Locations: Ruaka, Limuru Road, and surrounding Nairobi Metropolitan areas

We customize our management approach based on property type and owner's specific needs.

How do I list my property with you? +

Simple 3-Step Process:

  1. Contact Us: Call (0723544198 / 0716277844) or fill our contact form
  2. Property Inspection: Our team visits to assess the property and discuss your goals
  3. Agreement: Sign the management or letting agreement, and we get started

The process typically takes 2-3 days from initial contact to property going live in our marketing channels.

What are your management fees? +

Our fees are competitive and transparent:

  • Property Management: Typically 8-12% of monthly rent collected (varies by service level)
  • Lettings/Sales Brokerage: 5% of total transaction value
  • Rental Deposit Collection: 2% of deposit amount

For a detailed quote specific to your property, contact us with property details.

How often are inspections done? +

Our Inspection Schedule:

  • Regular Inspections: Quarterly (every 3 months) for ongoing properties
  • Initial Inspection: Upon tenant move-in (property condition documented)
  • Final Inspection: Upon tenant move-out (to assess any damages)
  • Emergency Inspections: Immediately upon notification of maintenance issues

You receive detailed inspection reports with photos and recommendations for any maintenance needed.

What happens when a tenant defaults on rent? +

Our Professional Arrears Management Process:

  1. Immediate Contact: We contact the tenant on the due date if payment not received
  2. Written Notice: Formal notice of arrears within 2 days
  3. Payment Plan Negotiation: We explore payment arrangements for genuine difficulties
  4. Legal Action: If unresolved after 30 days, we proceed with formal eviction process

We aim to recover rent and maintain tenant relationships when possible, but protect your income decisively.

How do I pay rent or report maintenance issues? +

Easy Payment & Communication Methods:

Rent Payment:

  • Mobile Money (M-Pesa)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Check Payment
  • Direct Deposit

Report Maintenance:

  • Call: 0723544198 / 0716277844 (for emergencies)
  • WhatsApp or Email: info@estatemasters.co.ke
  • Contact Form: Submit via our website

Include photos and detailed descriptions. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What do I need to know about tenant agreements? +

Key Agreement Terms:

  • Duration: Typically 12 months (flexible options available)
  • Rent & Payment: Monthly rent amount and due date
  • Security Deposit: Usually 1 month's rent (refundable)
  • Maintenance Responsibility: Clear division of landlord vs. tenant duties
  • Termination Notice: Typically 30-60 days from either party

We provide legally compliant agreements that protect both parties' interests.

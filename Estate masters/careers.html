Join Our Team

At Estate Masters, we believe great talent drives great results. We're looking for dedicated professionals who share our passion for excellence, innovation, and client success. Join a team where your work makes a real difference.

Why Work with Estate Masters?

Professional Growth

We invest in continuous learning and professional development. Access training programs, industry certifications, and mentorship from experienced real estate professionals.

Teamwork & Culture

Our environment values collaboration, respect, and mutual support. We celebrate wins together and solve challenges collectively.

Innovation & Initiative

Your ideas matter. We encourage innovation, reward initiative, and create an environment where you can take ownership of projects.

Career Advancement

Clear pathways for promotion and career growth. Grow with us as the company expands across Nairobi Metropolitan.

Current Job Openings

We regularly recruit dedicated professionals. Below are key areas where we hire:

Field Officers (Sales & Lettings)

Responsibilities:

  • Property viewings and client meetings
  • Tenant/buyer matching and negotiations
  • Market research and competitor analysis
  • Client relationship management
  • Transaction support and follow-up

Requirements: Valid driving license, excellent communication, sales mindset, local knowledge of Nairobi Metropolitan

Benefits: Base salary + attractive commissions, field allowance, training, vehicle support

Property Management Officers

Responsibilities:

  • Tenant screening and onboarding
  • Rent collection and follow-up
  • Property inspections and maintenance coordination
  • Rent arrears management
  • Tenant communication and complaint handling
  • Documentation and record-keeping

Requirements: Attention to detail, organizational skills, customer service excellence, problem-solving ability

Benefits: Competitive salary, training, performance bonuses, career growth

Accountants & Finance Officers

Responsibilities:

  • Financial record-keeping and reconciliation
  • Monthly financial statements preparation
  • Tax compliance and reporting
  • Budget management and forecasting
  • Client accounting reports

Requirements: Accounting qualification (CPA/KASNEB), bookkeeping experience, proficiency with accounting software, attention to accuracy

Benefits: Attractive salary, professional development, growth opportunities in finance

Marketing & Communications Officers

Responsibilities:

  • Social media content creation and management
  • Property photography and video production
  • Digital marketing campaigns
  • Website content management
  • Brand development and communications

Requirements: Creative skills, social media expertise, basic photography/video skills, digital marketing knowledge

Benefits: Competitive salary, creative freedom, modern tools, professional growth

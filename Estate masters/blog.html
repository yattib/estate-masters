Real Estate Insights & Resources

Expert tips, market insights, and practical advice for property owners, tenants, and investors. Stay informed about real estate trends in Nairobi Metropolitan.

Property Management Tips for Landlords

How to Reduce Vacancies Through Strategic Marketing

Key Strategies:

  • Multi-platform social media advertising (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
  • Professional photography and video walkthroughs
  • Competitive pricing based on local market trends
  • Quick response to inquiries within 24 hours
  • Online platforms and listing aggregators

Result: Properties are typically filled within 2-4 weeks with the right marketing approach.

Best Practices in Tenant Screening

Professional Tenant Selection:

  • Credit history and financial stability verification
  • Employment verification and income assessment
  • Rental history from previous landlords
  • Reference checks for reliability
  • ID verification and background checks

Benefit: Quality tenants reduce arrears, maintenance issues, and turnover costs.

Managing Rent Arrears Professionally

Professional Arrears Management:

  • Clear payment terms and communication
  • Early reminder system (before due date)
  • Payment plan negotiations for genuine difficulties
  • Legal action only as last resort
  • Transparent documentation throughout process

Result: Maintains relationships while protecting rental income.

Lease Administration Best Practices

Comprehensive Lease Management:

  • Clear, legally compliant lease agreements
  • Property condition documentation at move-in/out
  • Maintenance responsibility clarity
  • Transparent terms for repairs and improvements
  • Regular property inspections (quarterly or semi-annually)

Outcome: Fewer disputes and smooth tenant relationships.

Market Insights: Nairobi Metropolitan Real Estate

Apartment Pricing Trends (Ruaka & Surrounding)

Current Market Analysis:

  • Studio: KSH 10,000 - 16,000 per month
  • 1-Bedroom: KSH 18,000 - 28,000 per month
  • 2-Bedroom: KSH 30,000 - 50,000 per month
  • 3-Bedroom: KSH 45,000 - 75,000 per month

Prices vary based on proximity to main road, amenities, and property condition.

Commercial Space Rental Rates

Office & Business Space:

Commercial property is growing due to business expansion in the area.

Tenant Advice & Rights

Understanding Tenancy Agreements

Key Points Every Tenant Should Know:

  • Duration of lease (fixed term or periodic)
  • Monthly rent amount and payment due date
  • Security deposit terms and conditions
  • Maintenance responsibilities (landlord vs. tenant)
  • Notice period for termination (usually 30-60 days)
  • House rules and tenant conduct expectations

Tip: Always request a written lease agreement before moving in.

Reporting Maintenance Issues

How to Report Problems Effectively:

  • Report issues in writing (email or WhatsApp for proof)
  • Include clear photos of the problem
  • Give the landlord reasonable time to fix (5-7 days)
  • For emergency issues, call immediately
  • Keep records of all communications
  • Document work done and any disputes

Your Right: A property must be maintained in good, habitable condition.
